We're delighted to welcome our guests to Tuscany. There are many wonderful options for accommodation both in Florence and in the Chianti countryside near Greve. In Florence, you'll find a wide range of hotels, from elegant historic properties in the city center to charming boutique stays in quieter neighborhoods. Around Greve in Chianti, guests can enjoy peaceful agriturismi, vineyard villas, and cozy B&Bs surrounded by olive groves and vineyards. We recommend booking early, as this is a popular time of year for visitors to the region. Please find some personal favorites of ours listed:

  • ALBERGO DEL CHIANTI

  • PODERE CAMPRIANO

  • BORGO DEL CABREO

  • LA PIETRA DEL CABREO

  • PANZANELLO

  • VITICCIO

  • FATTORIA RIGNANA

  • FATTORIA SANTO STEFANO
